Hello, I’m Jill.

My home is Pawtucket, Rhode Island.

Background

I’m an experienced graphic designer, and not just in using the tools (which I am! — including the Adobe Creative Suite), but also in effectively managing the projects I work on. What makes me special as a designer is my ability to not only be creative, but to place a huge emphasis on customer service. From the very beginning of a project, I make a point to listen well, ask the right questions, and gain a big picture of a particular project’s goals, including how it might play a role in other initiatives that are established or upcoming.

I'm excited to bring these skills to web design, in addition to my experience in print.

Goals

I want to become a web designer, to supplement my skills in print design, and round out my all-around creative skills. For starters, I want a solid understanding of:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

I would like to be able to offer my clients a complete package of design know-how — from print to web.
